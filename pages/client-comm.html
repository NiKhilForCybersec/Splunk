"How do detections handle DDoS vs legitimate traffic spikes?" I'm implementing multi-signal correlation: source diversity analysis, geographic distribution, user agent patterns, and request entropy. A legitimate traffic spike from a marketing campaign shows different characteristics than a botnet attack â€” recognizable user agents, expected geographic distribution, and natural request patterns.

If Akamai logs are delayed beyond threshold â€” typically 30 minutes â€” an operational alert fires. The runbook includes escalation to the Splunk admin and Akamai support contacts.
