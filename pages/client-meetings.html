Professional Language Guide

âœ… Say This

  • "Based on my analysis of your historical data..."
  • "Industry best practice recommends..."
  • "I've implemented this using a statistical approach..."
  • "The detection leverages MITRE ATT&CK technique..."
  • "I recommend a phased approach to minimize risk..."
  • "Let me walk you through the detection logic..."
  • "I've documented this for knowledge transfer..."
  • "This aligns with your compliance requirements..."

âŒ Avoid This

  • "I think..." (instead: "Based on data/analysis...")
  • "It should work..." (instead: "Testing confirms...")
  • "I don't know" (instead: "Let me research that...")
  • "That's not my job" (instead: "I can coordinate with...")
  • "We've always done it this way" (be adaptable)
  • "This is easy/simple" (undermines complexity)
  • "I'm just a contractor" (own your expertise)
  • "Hopefully..." (instead: "The expected outcome...")
